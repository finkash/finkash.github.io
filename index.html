Zorica Nikov

Hello! I'm Zorica Nikov. I finished High School in the Jane Sandanski Gymnasium in Strumica with excellent success. I studied computer science in Skopje, which taught me how to think — but everything else taught me how to live.

I'm drawn to things that require patience and attention. Photography, because it forces you to slow down and really look at the world. Books and literature, because there is no faster way to live a hundred lives. Theater and classical music, because some emotions simply cannot be put into words — they have to be performed. I travel whenever I can, mostly because I find that the best version of myself tends to show up somewhere new. And I follow Formula 1 with perhaps an unreasonable level of dedication — though I'd argue there's no better lesson in strategy, pressure, and resilience than watching a race unfold. I like the space where logic and creativity meet. That's where I feel most at home. On success and happiness — a few things I genuinely believe: Success comes slowly, then all at once. Be patient with the slow part. Happiness is almost never where you expect it. It tends to show up in small things — a good sentence, a quiet morning, music that catches you off guard. Find at least one thing you follow with unreasonable passion — it keeps you human.

"A person who does nothing will enjoy no happiness." - V.
